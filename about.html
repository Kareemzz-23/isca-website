Who are we?

The UMW-ISCA is an international student association for cardiology that aims to provide students an opportunity to partake in various research like meta-analyses, case studies, systemic reviews, as well as clinical rotations, and student-led presentations.

What can we offer you?

Joining UMW-ISCA is a great way to gain controlled exposure to clinical settings in cardiology for all levels and all years. It also provides a strong foundation for learning ECGs, resuscitation, and a comprehensive understanding of hemodynamics that are crucial for patient management in many specialties.

Research

UMW-ISCA is working very closely with the cardiology research club and the Polish branch of the European Society of Cardiology (ESC). We attend and hold conferences, where students present research to a committee of staff members who supervise research and provide critical analyses and recommendations.

The team

Apart from being a place where medical students can take part in research, attend conferences, shadow on-duty cardiologists, and develop an interest and prowess in cardiology, UMW-ISCA is a great place to meet like-minded, ambitious, and friendly medical students who are happy to guide new members through some of the learning and activities. UMW-ISCA meetings have a great atmosphere, and joining UMW-ISCA is a massive opportunity for medical students to enjoy their studies and benefit from them.
