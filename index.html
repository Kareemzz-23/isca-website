About Us

The International Student's Cardiology Association (ISCA) is a community dedicated to connecting future cardiologists worldwide. We aim to foster collaboration, share resources, and provide opportunities for education and innovation in the field of cardiology.

Our mission is to empower students with knowledge, mentorship, and hands-on experience to shape the future of cardiovascular medicine.
